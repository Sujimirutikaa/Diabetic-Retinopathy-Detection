‚ÑπÔ∏è About Diabetic Retinopathy Stages

Stage 0 - No DR (Healthy): No signs of diabetic retinopathy. The retina appears normal with no visible damage from diabetes.
Stage 1 - Mild DR: Presence of microaneurysms only. Small areas of balloon-like swelling in the retina's tiny blood vessels.
Stage 2 - Moderate DR: More than just microaneurysms but less than severe. May include dot and blot hemorrhages and hard exudates.
Stage 3 - Severe DR: Multiple hemorrhages and microaneurysms in all four retinal quadrants, plus venous beading or prominent microvascular abnormalities.
Stage 4 - Proliferative DR: Advanced stage with new abnormal blood vessel formation (neovascularization) and possible fibrous proliferation.
